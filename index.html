About Cancer Ciller

Liver cancer is one of the most prevalent cancers in Taiwan. It is also one of the top ten cancers in the United States. Cancer Ciller is a game that would guide you through the methods of treating liver cancers and what you would need to consider when you are a liver cancer victim. While killing the cancer cells in Cancer Ciller, you would also gain more knowledge of medicine and all sorts of therapies, especially ones that are targeted for liver cancers.

About Me - Shiloh Liu

My father is an interventional radiologist who does minimally-invasive image-guided procedures to diagnose and treat different diseases. He is specialised in treating the abdomen, especially for liver cancers such as hepatitis. I am exposed to this kind of knowledge from a very young age. I am also extremely interested in what my father is doing and after quite a long talk with my father, I decided to make XXX, combining my passion for biology and coding both. My goal is to let more people, including you, know more about liver cancer, its treatments, and the importance of making the right decision.

What Happened?

Taiwan is a country with a high percentage of patients who have liver cancers. Today you are here as a doctor in the NTUH, the National Taiwan University Hospital, and you are faced with a liver cancer patient on the stage. Your goal is to try to kill the cancer cells and cure the patient. Saving the patient is your first priority.
